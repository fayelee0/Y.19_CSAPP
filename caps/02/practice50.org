* Practice 50

  xx.0{0|1}{00|01|10|11}
  00, 01 round-down
  11,    round-up
  10,    if prev 1 + 1, else +0, because 10 = 0.5
  

** A  

   10.010(2)
   = 10.01 {00} = 10.01


** B

   10.011(2)
   = 10.01 {10} = 10.10


** C

   10.110(2)
   = 10.11 {00} = 10.11


** D

   11.001(2)
   = 11.00 {10} = 11.00
